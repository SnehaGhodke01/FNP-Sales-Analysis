üîç Key Insights
1. Revenue by Occasions & Categories
Top Occasion: Raksha Bandhan stands out as the highest revenue-generating occasion, followed by Anniversaries.

Top Categories: Sweets and Soft Toys are the leading categories by revenue, while Mugs and Cakes show lower comparative totals.

2. Temporal Trends
Monthly Performance: There is a significant spike in revenue during August, likely correlated with the Raksha Bandhan festival.

Hourly Peaks: Order volume peaks significantly around 7:00 PM (19:00 hrs), suggesting high evening customer activity.

3. Geographical & Product Performance
Top Cities: Haridwar, Imphal, and Kavali are among the top cities by order volume.

Top Product: "Dolores Gift" is the highest-grossing individual product among the top five.
